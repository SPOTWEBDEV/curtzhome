Our Journey

Seventeen Years of Measured Growth

Since our founding, Curtz Home has grown from a single-city brokerage into one of the USA's most recognised real estate and investment firms — a trajectory built not on speculation, but on disciplined execution.

2008

Foundation

Curtz Home was incorporated in New York with a mandate to provide transparent, client-focused real estate brokerage services to the USA's emerging middle and upper class.

2012

Washington D.C. Expansion

Our second regional office opened in Washington D.C., granting clients access to the capital's rapidly developing residential and commercial corridors.

2016

Launch of Investment Division

Recognising client demand for structured wealth management, we introduced our tiered investment plan portfolio — delivering consistent double-digit returns since inception.

2020

Digital Platform Launch

The CurtzHome.com portal launched, enabling seamless online property browsing, investment subscriptions, and client account management from any device.

2025

West Coast & Midwest Expansion

New offices in Los Angeles and Chicago extend our national footprint, bringing our premium real estate services to two of the USA's fastest-growing economies.
